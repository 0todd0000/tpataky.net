Overview¶

Research focusses on modeling and analyzing the manifestation of stochastic processes in biomechanical systems. We place equal emphasis on applied continuum statistics, biomechanical modeling, and experimental human movement analysis.

Keywords:

  • Statistical Parametric Mapping
  • Random Field Theory
  • Continuum Registration
  • Probabilistic simulation

Lab Organization¶

  • Motion Analysis Group
  • Golf Group
  • FEA Group

Each research group is directly supported by external funding and both internal and external research collaborators.
